Traning philosopy

We organise single-double dives and half or full diving tips.We dive in small groups usual ratio one dive guide/4 divers.We have 6 courses1.Open water diver,dive masters.instructors will focus on developing coordination,flexibility and strength while learning.



Our classes

We are happy to announce that we are going launch swim to dive classes starting 2018.This program is to teach Fundemental of all aquatic sports,and ensure water safety to children from 6-13 years old.

Begineer class is designed for 6-10 years old who would like to learn to swim.Advance class is designed for 10-13 years old who would like to continue to develop their swimming skills and basic diving skills.

Future champions class is designed to provide students with a foundation for elite performance in sport.Each 75 minutes class include 30 minutes of stretching,tumbling and trampoline training and 45 minutes of diving.
