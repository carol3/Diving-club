Dive sites

The reefs along the kenya coast are extremely diverse and all standards of diving can b found to suit even the most fastidious diver.



Mwaka

A nice slope with some intersting coral formations.keep your eyes open for turtles which are often seen resting here.Boat Ride:20min average.A very different reef.Reef lop at 27 meters with drop offs to 50meters.Dense growths of whip coral and sea fans.

Maweni

The southern part of the reef is a caral drop off which gradually softens.at one point of the dive one dives oner a large field of sea anemone's which makes this one of our most pleasant dives.

Kingi

There is an exciting coral head on the reef that of five has huge amount of small reef fish.We often see most of the rarer fish.
